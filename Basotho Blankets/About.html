History of the Company

Our factory, Proud Nation is the oldest blanket manufacturer in South Africa/Lesotho established in 1959 and is located in Mohale's Hoek just around the town . It is a 4th generation, locally owned, family business. Proud Nation is the exclusive manufacturer of Basotho Heritage Blankets, with the blessing of the Basotho Nation. We have been successfully supported by Basotho since the Start of the Business and It is our warmly pleasure to serve all Basotho with Peace and Honesty! As the Company which is legally registered, We are very proud to earn all Basotho trust on selling this unique African Brand to them.

Aims

  • To provide high-quality, comfortable blankets to customers at an affordable price.
  • To offer a wide variety of blankets in different colors, sizes, materials, and designs to meet the diverse needs and preferences of customers.
  • To ensure that all blankets are made from safe, durable, and eco-friendly materials that are ethically sourced and produced.

Objectives

  • Increase sales revenue by a certain percentage over the next year, by expanding the customer base and increasing average order value.
  • Improve customer satisfaction by achieving a customer satisfaction rating of 90% or above, through surveys, feedback mechanisms, and customer service training.
  • Develop new blanket products that meet emerging customer needs, such as blankets for pets, travel, or outdoor activities.
